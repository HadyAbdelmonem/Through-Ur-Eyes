Anemia

The symptoms of anemia

1)Feeling extreme fatigue and weakness.

2)Dizziness, lightheadedness, and fainting.

3)Difficulty concentrating and remembering.

4)Feeling sluggish, lazy, and inactive.

5)Headaches and body aches.

6)Loss of appetite and weight loss.

7)Feeling cold and experiencing numbness in the extremities.

Hypertensive

The symptoms of Hypertensive.

1)Severe and constant headache.

2)vertigo, and loss of balance.

3)Feeling extreme fatigue and weakness.

4)Feeling anxious, tense, and confused.

5)Chest pain and difficulty breathing.

6)Excessive nosebleeds.

7)Increased and irregular heart rate.

Diabetes

The symptoms of Diabetes.

1)Excessive thirst and dry mouth.

2)Feeling excessively hungry and having a constant desire to eat.

3)Frequent and heavy urination, especially at night.

4)Feeling extreme fatigue and weakness.

5)Feeling dizzy and having headaches.

6)Feeling itchy with dry skin.

7)Frequent urinary and skin infections.
